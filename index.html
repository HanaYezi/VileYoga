About Hannah


I've been practicing yoga here and there for many years, but have had a daily practice since about 2013. After college I spent four years abroad in Shanghai, China and had the good luck to practice under several incredible teachers there. They were regularly going to India for more trainings so in 2015 I decided to follow them. I enjoyed it very much, but felt like I had only gotten my feet wet. Since then I've continued a regular Ashtanga & Kriya Yoga practice that I feel very blessed to get to share with my own friends and stundents.
