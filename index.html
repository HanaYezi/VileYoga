    About Hannah Vile

    I've been practicing yoga several years, & have had a daily practice since about 2013. After college I moved abroad to Shanghai, China and had the good luck to practice under some really incredible teachers there. They were regularly going to India for more trainings so in 2015 I decided to follow them.

    I really enjoyed it, but felt like I had only gotten my feet wet. Since then I've continued a regular Ashtanga & Kriya Yoga practice that I feel very blessed to get to share with my own friends and students.

    Ashtanga

    Ashtanga is a set sequence of postures popularized by K. Pattabhi Jois.

    It's usually practiced with no music or music without a melody. There are a total of 6 series of Ashtanga, each with increasing dificulty.

    We make an effort to generally stick to the sequence, but there's alot of variation in indivual's bodies.

    And since most people will spend years becoming fully proficient in the primary sequence alone, I grant alot of new posture allowances for the sake of fun.
